About

Who We Are

Mate Nation is an independent record label built on friendship, community, and a genuine belief in emerging talent. We are more than a label — we are a training ground where upcoming artists get the tools, mentorship, and real studio experience they need to build lasting careers in music.

Mate Nation is more than a record label — it's a movement built on friendship, community, and the belief that every artist deserves a real shot at their dreams.

We exist to bridge the gap between raw talent and professional artistry. We work with upcoming and emerging artists who have the fire but need the platform, the guidance, and the infrastructure to make their mark.

As a training ground for trainees , we invest in artists at every stage of their journey. Whether you're recording your first track or preparing to drop your debut project, Mate Nation is where you grow.

The Vision Behind It

Our Founder

Mate Nation was founded by Arunav Khanra , widely known as AVIK — a multi-genre music producer, label manager, and the Director of Soundwave Music Group. Active since 2020, AVIK has worked with artists and labels worldwide. His vision is to keep real human talent alive and supported in a rapidly changing, AI-driven music industry.

Arunav Khanra — AVIK Multi-Genre Producer Director — Soundwave Music Group Active Since 2020

Mate Nation was founded by Arunav Khanra , known across the industry as AVIK — a multi-genre producer, manager, and the Director of Soundwave Music Group. Since 2020, AVIK has worked with artists and labels worldwide, producing across genres and building careers from the ground up.

He created Mate Nation with a single, clear intention: to build a label that treats artists as people first — not products. A place where the emerging and the untested are given real tools, real time, and real support to develop into the artists they are meant to be.

Why Mate Nation Exists

The music industry changes fast. AVIK built this label to be a stable, human-first space — a training ground and a home — for artists who need more than just a deal. They need a team that believes in them.

Training Ground

How We Build Artists

Mate Nation's structured training programme takes artists from raw talent to professional release-ready performers. Through discovery, hands-on development, studio production, strategic release planning, and long-term career growth support, we invest in every artist at every stage of their journey. This is what separates us from a typical record deal.

01

Discovery

We find raw talent wherever it lives — social media, open mics, direct submissions. If you've got it, we'll see it.

02

Development

Trainees enter a structured development program: vocal coaching, songwriting workshops, studio technique, and performance training.

03

Production

Access to professional studio time, in-house producers, and a network of engineers to shape your sound into something release-ready.

04

Release

Strategic rollout across all major platforms with marketing support, playlist pitching, and press coverage behind your music.

05

Growth

We don't drop artists after one release. We build careers — long-term, with consistent support, guidance, and reinvestment.
